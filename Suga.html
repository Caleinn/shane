Min Yoon-gi (Suga)

Overview

Min Yoon-gi, known as Suga, is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, and record producer, and a member of the globally renowned boy band BTS.

Key Details

  • Birth Date: March 9, 1993
  • Birth Place: Daegu, South Korea
  • Position in BTS: Lead rapper
  • Stage Names: Suga (when performing with BTS), Agust D (when releasing solo music)

Career Highlights

  • Debut with BTS: Suga debuted with BTS in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment (now known as HYBE Corporation).
  • Solo Work: He has released solo mixtapes, including "Agust D" in 2016 and "D-2" in 2020, showcasing his versatility as an artist.
  • Songwriting and Production: In addition to his rapping, Suga is known for his songwriting and production skills, contributing to many of BTS's hits and collaborating with various artists across different genres.

Personal Traits

  • Musical Style: Suga is recognized for his introspective lyrics, often addressing themes such as mental health, personal struggles, and social issues. His production style blends various genres, including hip-hop, R&B, and pop.
  • Public Persona: He is often seen as the "mood maker" of BTS, known for his witty humor and candid personality.

Interests

Suga is passionate about music and enjoys producing tracks both for himself and for other artists. He is also a fan of basketball and video games.
