Jeon Jung-kook (전정국)

Basic Information

  • Birthdate: September 1, 1997
  • Birthplace: Busan, South Korea
  • Position in BTS: Main vocalist, lead dancer, and maknae (youngest member)

Career Highlights

  • Debut: Jungkook debuted with BTS in June 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. The group quickly rose to fame and has become one of the biggest music acts in the world.
  • Musical Style: Known for his versatile vocals, Jungkook is skilled in various music genres, including pop, R&B, and hip-hop.
  • Songwriting and Producing: Apart from performing, Jungkook has contributed to writing and producing several songs for BTS and his solo projects.

Solo Projects

  • "Euphoria": Released in 2018, this song is one of his most popular solo tracks and showcases his vocal range.
  • "Still With You": A song released in 2020 as part of BTS's "Map of the Soul: 7" album, which he co-wrote.

Achievements

  • Awards: As part of BTS, Jungkook has received numerous awards, including Billboard Music Awards, American Music Awards, and more. He also won several awards for his individual performances.
  • Influence: Jungkook is known for his strong fan connection, often referred to as ARMY, and has a significant influence on social media.

Personal Interests

  • Hobbies: He enjoys playing video games, drawing, and sports. He is also known for his love of food and cooking.
  • Talents: Jungkook is skilled in various forms of dance and has showcased his talents through different performances, including choreographed dance covers.

Fun Facts

  • Nicknames: He is often called "Kookie" by fans and members of BTS.
  • Tattoos: Jungkook has several tattoos that hold personal meaning for him.
