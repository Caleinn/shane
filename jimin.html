Jimin (Park Ji-min)

Basic Information

  • Birthdate: October 13, 1995
  • Birthplace: Busan, South Korea
  • Position in BTS: Lead vocalist and main dancer
  • Education: He studied at Busan High School of Arts and later transferred to Korea Arts High School, where he focused on modern dance.

Career Highlights

  • Debut: Jimin debuted with BTS on June 13, 2013.
  • Musical Style: He is known for his emotive vocal delivery and powerful dance performances, often incorporating contemporary dance elements into his routines.
  • Songwriting and Producing: Jimin has contributed to writing and producing several songs for BTS. Notable tracks include “Lie,” “Serendipity,” and “Filter.”

Solo Projects

  • Solo Songs: Jimin has released solo songs within BTS’s discography, such as:
    • “Lie” (from the album WINGS)
    • “Serendipity” (from the album Love Yourself: Answer)
    • “Filter” (from the album Map of the Soul: 7)
  • Collaborations: He has collaborated with other artists, including the song “With You” with Ha Sung-woon for the Our Blues OST.

Achievements

  • Awards: As a member of BTS, Jimin has won numerous awards, including Billboard Music Awards and American Music Awards. His individual performances have also received recognition.
  • Influence: Jimin is known for his strong stage presence and has a significant following on social media, often referred to as "Moons," which reflects the love and admiration from his fans.

Personal Interests

  • Hobbies: Jimin enjoys dancing, singing, and trying out new foods. He is known for his love of cooking and often shares his culinary experiences with fans.
  • Personality: He is often described as warm-hearted and caring, frequently expressing love and gratitude towards his fans and fellow members.

Fun Facts

  • Nicknames: Jimin is affectionately called “Jiminnie” and “Mochi” by fans and his fellow members.
  • Tattoo: Jimin has a tattoo that reads "Nevermind," which reflects his personal philosophy and connection to the song of the same name.
