Kim Nam-joon (RM)

Basic Information

Birthdate: September 12, 1994

Role in BTS: Leader, Main Rapper

Position

RM is known for his exceptional rap skills, deep voice, and insightful lyrics. He plays a crucial role in BTS's songwriting and production.

Education

He attended Global Cyber University, where he majored in Broadcasting and Entertainment.

Solo Work

RM has released solo projects, including the mixtapes "RM" and "Indigo." His solo work often reflects his personal thoughts and experiences.

Interests

RM is an avid reader, enjoys art, and has a passion for language. He is fluent in English, which he learned through self-study and practice.

Personality

He is known for his intelligence, thoughtfulness, and leadership skills. RM often speaks about social issues and mental health, showing a deep understanding of the world around him.
