Kim Seok-jin (Jin)

Basic Information

Birthdate: December 4, 1992

Role in BTS: Vocalist, Visual

Position

Jin is known for his strong vocal abilities and charming personality. He is often referred to as the "visual" of the group due to his striking looks.

Education

He studied at Konkuk University, majoring in Film Studies.

Solo Work

Jin has released solo tracks, including "Awake," "Epiphany," and "Yours." He is known for his emotional delivery and has contributed significantly to BTS's discography.

Interests

He enjoys cooking, playing video games, and is known for his love of food, particularly his favorite dish, sushi.

Personality

Jin is often described as caring, playful, and a bit of a jokester, earning him the nickname "Worldwide Handsome" due to his good looks and charm.
