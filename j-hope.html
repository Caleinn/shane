J-Hope (Jung Ho-seok)

Key Details

  • Birth Date: February 18, 1994
  • Birth Place: Gwangju, South Korea
  • Position in BTS: Lead dancer, sub-rapper
  • Stage Name: J-Hope

Career Highlights

  • Debut with BTS: J-Hope debuted with BTS in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment (now known as HYBE Corporation).
  • Solo Work: He released his solo mixtape, "Hope World," in 2018, which received critical acclaim and showcased his versatility as an artist.
  • Dance Skills: J-Hope is known for his exceptional dancing ability, often being regarded as the best dancer in BTS. He has a background in street dance and has participated in various dance competitions.
  • Songwriting and Production: He has contributed to many of BTS's tracks, often incorporating his personal experiences and messages of hope and positivity.

Personal Traits

  • Musical Style: J-Hope's music often blends hip-hop, R&B, and pop, with themes focusing on self-empowerment, dreams, and resilience.
  • Public Persona: He is known for his bright and energetic personality, often acting as the "sunshine" of BTS. His optimism and infectious energy make him a beloved figure among fans.
